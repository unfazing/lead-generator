## Decisions Made

- `/enrich` now owns mutable batch management and member ingestion so enrichment is no longer anchored to a specific people recipe page.
- Saved people snapshots remain the only allowed source of new batch members, preserving the reviewed-snapshot constraint from earlier phases.
- The people workflow keeps row selection for review context, but the actual add-to-batch action only executes inside `/enrich`.
